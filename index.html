FORTIFIED Roof / Strengthen Alabama Homes

Property & Inspector Info
Required
Exterior Elevation Photos
Photos
Capture one photo from each face of the dwelling (front, back, left, right). Photos are auto-stamped with GPS & timestamp.
Foundation
Eligibility
Foundations must not be "dry stack"(unrestrained stacked masonry/stone with no foundation).
Roof Framing & Rafter Spacing
Eligibility
Rafters min 2x6 at ≤24" o.c., or trusses min 2x4 at ≤24" o.c. Greater spacing requires engineered solution.
Roof Deck (Type & Thickness)
Eligibility
Min 7/16" plywood/OSB, continuous dimensional lumber, or T&G decking. 3/8" does not qualify — must be redecked.
Roof Deck Attachment
Eligibility
Required: 8d ring shank nails ≤4" o.c. (12 nails per 4 feet). For T&G or 1x decking: add one nail (8d ring shank) to each board <6" wide and two nails to boards >6" wide. When replacing damaged plywood/OSB, replace the entire sheet — do not patch.
Sealed Roof Deck
Eligibility
Sealing the roof deck guards against roof covering damage that could allow water intrusion into the interior. If roof is in like-new condition, closed-cell polyurethane spray foam may be installed inside attic at all joints between sheathing and at intersections between sheathing and roof framing members, and at all valleys. NOTE: closed-cell foam is NOT permitted with wood board roof decking (T&G or 1x planks).
Roof Covering
Eligibility
When RETROFIT REQUIRED, replace roof covering with a high wind resistant roof covering. Remove existing covering down to deck, replace rotted wood. Seal the deck and re-nail with 8d ring shank nails ≤4" o.c., replace all flashing at walls, chimneys, dormers, valleys, boots, vent hoods, drip edges.

1. Shingles: ASTM D 3161 Class F AND ASTM D 7158 Class H.
2. Metal Panels: direct-to-deck, no battens, engineered installation drawings (fastener type, size, spacing for all roof areas including 4 ft from any roof edge) required.
3. Metal Shingles: install per manufacturer’s written instructions for high wind exposures.
4. All other coverings: provide Product Approval and installation instructions demonstrating compliance with high wind standards prior to selecting the roof covering.
Roof Ventilation
Eligibility
Ridge / off-ridge / power / static vents must meet TAS 100(A). Sealed attics: no retrofit required.
Gable Walls & Vents
Eligibility
When RETROFIT REQUIRED, gable vents must be permanently sealed OR have removable covers installed to prevent water intrusion during high wind events. Covers can be plywood panels, but the anchoring system (e.g. hanger bolts) must be permanently installed 16" o.c. on the two longer opposing sides of each opening. (See Drawing F-GS-1)
Attached Structures (Porches/Carports)
Eligibility
Photos required: each side of structure, on top of roof showing connection to house, slope of roof, and space beneath. If porch roof cover must be included in reroof, select Retrofit Required.
Opening Protection (Silver)
Eligibility
Windows & glazed doors: ASTM E 1886+E 1996 Missile Level D. Bay windows trigger retrofit.
Roof Slopes
Photos
Capture a photo of EVERY roof slope. Note all slope measurements — multiple slopes may require separate engineering review.
Attic Overview Photos
Photos
4+overall photos of attic area, plus close-ups of decking, fasteners, and any visible damage.
Site Conditions
Required
Saltwater shoreline distance (TB FH 2021-02) and wind zone drive material requirements.
Special Eligibility (Disqualifiers)
Eligibility
PV / rooftop HVAC / elevated decks require engineer letters. Dry-stack foundation disqualifies the home.
Drip Edge
Eligibility
FORTIFIED requires drip edge at all eaves & rakes when re-roofing (Section 4.5).
Flashing
Eligibility
All flashing must be replaced when re-roofing (Section 4.6) — perimeters, pitch changes, penetrations, chimneys, valleys, drains.
Soffits
Silver
Silver: flexible soffits need intermediate bracing per F-S-1. Within 3000 ft saltwater: corrosion-resistant fasteners (Section 5.6).
Chimney
Silver
Silver: 1 exterior photo+2 attic photos per chimney showing framing & anchorage (Section 5.7).
Entry & Garage Doors
Silver
Hurricane Silver: entry/garage doors need impact rating. Garage doors also need design pressure rating. 3 photos per garage door (Sections 5.3, 5.4).
Roofer Certification
2025 Req
As of Nov 1, 2025, the 2025 FORTIFIED Standard requires IBHS-certified roofing contractors. Verify before scheduling work.
